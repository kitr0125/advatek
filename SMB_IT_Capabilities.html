What We Watch Every Week
👤
Administrator Account Audit
Every Monday we verify the local Administrators group on every workstation. Any unexpected account — one that wasn't put there by us — triggers an immediate alert. We also confirm the built-in Administrator and Guest accounts remain disabled.
🔄
Patch Compliance
We check when Windows last installed updates, how many updates are pending, and whether any are rated Critical or Important by Microsoft. A machine that hasn't patched in 30 days gets a ticket. Pending security updates get a higher priority alert.
🛡️
Antivirus Health
We verify Bitdefender GravityZone is running, registered with Windows, and definitions are no more than 3 days old. A stopped agent or stale definitions triggers an immediate alert and we attempt automatic recovery.
🔐
Failed Login Detection
We scan the security event log for failed login attempts, account lockouts, and unusual authentication patterns. More than 10 failed attempts on a single account in a week gets flagged — more than 25 is treated as a potential brute-force attack.
💾
System Health & Disk Space
We check disk space on every drive, memory availability, CPU load, pending reboots, and uptime. A drive below 15% gets a warning. Below 5% gets a critical alert. Machines that haven't rebooted in 30 days get flagged — pending updates may not have applied.
📡
RMM Agent Health
Our system health check also verifies the Syncro RMM agent itself is running on every managed machine. If the agent stops, we lose visibility into that machine. We detect it and attempt automatic recovery before the gap in monitoring grows.
